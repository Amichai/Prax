About Us

Our Mission:

Praxiteles Algorithm Designs LLC (Prax) is an algorithm and software development company that is designing and developing high-quality pattern recognition software.

Our Method:

Amichai Levy and Sammy Steiner assembled a team of ambitious, innovative, and resourceful problem solvers and programmers to spearhead innovation for a new generation of human-computer interface and algorithmic capabilities. Prax emphasizes creativity, cooperation, and inspiration to effectively collaborate and efficiently develop great software.

Our Software:

Prax’s pattern recognition algorithm offers an exciting new approach to the problem of pattern recognition. Prax’s algorithm can be extended to image, video and speech recognition, effectively teaching computers and devices to understand real world patterns and complex forms of input.
